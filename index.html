About Me

I am a Research Fellow at the High Performance Computing Lab, School of Civil Engineering, University of Tehran, under the supervision of Professor Soheil Mohammadi.

My research focuses on developing computational frameworks for complex engineering problems, including:

  • Finite element analysis of coupled multiphysics reaction-diffusion equations
  • 3D multiscale topology optimization for composite structures
  • Machine learning applications in engineering and material science
  • Inverse design of mechanical metamaterials
  • High-performance computing and parallel processing
